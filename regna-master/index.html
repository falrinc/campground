What is the Santa Fe River Retreat?

Come experience the wonder of more than 30 natural springs, bubbling up from underground. A beautiful secluded campsite located near Poe Springs on the south bank of the Santa Fe River. Many are just a short walk from your campsite! Sprawling across 21 acres of an undisturbed natural long leaf pine and wire grass ecosystem, which are rarely found anywhere else in Florida. Awaken the adventurer within, and go for a ride down the gently winding river, go swimming in the cool, refreshing water at one of the several springs, or just relax on your own raised deck and observe the beauty of nature unfolding all around you.

Amenities

For a relaxing and worry-free stay, we offer many luxury camping amenities free of charge.

Raised Deck

The campsite features a 7ft x 7ft raised deck for keeping tents and sleeping bags off the ground, as well as offering a great spot for viewing the nature around you. The deck sleeps 5-6 adults comfortably.

Air Mattresses

Camp in ultimate comfort with our battery operated, full size air mattresses.
***Pillows not included***

Gas Grill

Many of our guest have heralded the gas grill as a superior amenity.
William, from Gainesville says, "Many times cooking is the most difficult and time-consuming part of camping; they've made it a non-factor by having a gas grill."

Water Vessels and Gear

We offer 3 different vessels for exploring the nearby river. A giant 16ft canoe that seats 3, a 16ft kayak that seats 2, and a 17ft ocean kayak for one.

Lessons/Guided Tours

Lessons and/or Guided Tours are available on weekends for an additional cost (See Reservations for more info).

River Access

Begin your Santa Fe River adventure in Poe Spring, located directly next to the campsite. In this area, you will see many naturally occurring outlets for the nearby springs.
